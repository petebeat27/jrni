April Fools

Pallet pbLogs

Tbh it's a chill day. I've always loved the fool card, it represents hope to me, and today has been a very foolish day when looking through that lens. Hope can get you places you never thought you'd be. Hope can open you up to things you'd never thought of. Hope brings a freedom, and an openess to the mind, body, and soul. Things go my way when I'm going a way at all, like the sun setting as I walk west.

Hope is limitless. I think a lot about the kind of man I want to be, and the kind of man in myself, as a child, I have seen.

I feel the growing pains no longer just physicaly. There is a certain pain I've been made aware of when ego grows. I think about position of the soul within the heart and mind. The position I am within myself. I could stay within the roots of my internal world, where knowldge grows like a system of roots, a growth that I've found in the coldness of life. Soil is a fertile darkness that births the life itself that we live in, that we are, that we think about. I love that I can grow from these roots, take position that is fixed, take in light, and let go.
